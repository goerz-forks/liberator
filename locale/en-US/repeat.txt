section:Macros[+++macros+++]

q {arg}
    record a keysequence into a macro
Available macros are {0-9a-zA-Z} (uppercase to append).type 'q' to stop recording.
    q
[count]@ {arg}
    Execute the contents of macro {0-9a-z}. @@ repeats the previous @{0-9a-z}
    @
